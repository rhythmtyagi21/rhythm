Got questions?
I've got answers.

What makes you passionate about engineering? –

Solving real-world problems with tech and making systems that actually help people is what fuels me.

What's your edge? –

Embedded systems, signal processing, and making things work that most people give up on. Also — smooth PCB layouts and clean MATLAB models.

Why photography? –

It’s my way of freezing emotions and scenes. With my Nikon D5600, I turn everyday moments into visual stories.

Where do you want to work next? –

Somewhere I can combine embedded tech, signal logic, and meaningful impact. Collaborations welcome!

How do you handle complex technical challenges? –

We break them down, simulate what we can, and iterate fast. Whether it's debugging embedded firmware or refining a signal model, we’re all about structured problem-solving.

What tools are you most comfortable with? –

STM32CubeIDE, MATLAB/Simulink, Eagle, Altium, Arduino, and even low-level C for microcontrollers. If it speaks binary, we’re in.

What's your creative process like? –

For engineering: sketch → simulate → prototype → refine. For photography: feel the vibe, frame it, shoot with intent. Either way, it’s all about storytelling.

What inspires you outside of work? –

Travel, trying new foods, and connecting with people. We believe fresh perspectives fuel better designs—technical and creative.
